How do I write an OpenEJB FAQ?

Just use a document like this one!

faq_music.xml
<?xml version="1.0"?>
<faqs>
    <super id="faq">FAQ</super>
    <super id="faq_music"></super>
    <name>OpenEJB with Music</name>

<faq>
<question>Do you know the sofware has a bug in it?</question> <answer 
id="life"> <p>
No, but if you hum a few bars, maybe I can fake it.
</p> 
</answer>
</faq>

       
<faq>
<question>How do I get to Carnagie Hall?</question>
<answer id="life">
<p>
Practice, man! Practice.
</p> 
</answer>
</faq>

       
</faqs>

Where do I send my FAQ?

Just submit it to the OpenEJB Issue Tracking System, it will be up in no time!
