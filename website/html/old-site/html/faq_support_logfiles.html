Finding OpenEJB Log Files

OpenEJB log files are one of the best ways of uncovering the cause of a problem. The most important log files to look at are the openejb.log files.

 NOTE
The log4j configuration will rotate the log files once they get larger than 100 Kbytes. These log files will be sufixed with an integer, e.g. openejb.log.1. It may be helpful to include all of the openejb.log files when submitting a support request.
